Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add the bot?

Click the "Add to Discord" button on any of our feature pages and follow the authorization process.

How to configure permissions?

Use the !setup command in your server to configure bot permissions and features.

Music quality issues?

Ensure the bot has proper permissions and try adjusting the audio settings using !music settings.

Ticket system setup?

Use !ticket setup to configure categories and support roles for your server.
